Client: Joint Improvised-Threat Defeat Organization (JIDO)

Project: JIDO Enterprise Acquisition Strategy Initiative

Dollar Value: $3 Billion

JIDO enables Department of Defense (DoD) actions to counter improvised threats with tactical responsiveness and anticipatory acquisition supporting Combatant Commands' efforts to prepare for, and adapt to, battlefield surprise working with counter-terrorism (CT), counter-insurgency (COIN), and other related mission areas including counter-improvised explosive device (C-IED). The JIDO Full Operational Capability (FOC) is scheduled for 1 October 2016. One of the initial operation capability criteria is a “contract strategy approved and in execution”. In order to streamline and simplify the acquisition of contract support services, JIDO established the Enterprise Acquisition Strategy Initiative (EASI). EASI is a strategy to procure seven performance-based task order contracts against GSA’s One Acquisition Solution for Integrated Services (OASIS) Small Business, OASIS Unrestricted, and Alliant contracts.
