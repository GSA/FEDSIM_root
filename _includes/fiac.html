Federal Industry Acquisition Council (FIAC)

AAS, through FEDSIM created the Federal Industry Acquisition Council, commonly referred to as the "FIAC", to ensure ongoing communication with industry leaders. The FIAC brings together members of the government contracting community and AAS leadership to provide an invaluable forum for open dialogue within the federal acquisition industry.

The FIAC has three committees. If you are interested in joining, select registration to open the Interact page.

Streamlined Acquisition

Focuses on improving the procurement process and overall FEDSIM experience, whether through policy, best practices, or process change. Select registration to open the Interact page.

Small Business

Focuses on engaging the small business community, across all GSA opportunities, then either matchmaking with large businesses or as outreach to the federal GOVCON small business community.

Marketing / Outreach

Focuses on educating the federal market on GSA AAS FEDSIM, sharing successes, promoting competition, and fostering awareness of FEDSIM's capabilities.
