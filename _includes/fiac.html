Federal Industry Acquisition Council (FIAC)

FEDSIM created the Federal Industry Acquisition Council, commonly referred to as the "FIAC", to ensure ongoing communication with industry leaders. The FIAC brings together members of the government contracting community and AAS leadership to provide an invaluable forum for open dialogue within the federal acquisition industry.

The FIAC has three active committees. If you interested in joining, please complete the FIAC Registration form below.

Streamlined Acquisition

Focuses on improving the procurement process and overall FEDSIM experience, whether through policy, best practices, or process change.

Small Business

Focuses on engaging the small business community, whether through GSA, AAS, FEDSIM opportunities, matchmaking with large businesses, or as outreach to the federal GOVCON small business community.

Marketing / Outreach

Focuses on educating the federal market on GSA AAS FEDSIM, sharing successes, promoting competition, and fostering awareness of FEDSIM's capabilities.
