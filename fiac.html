FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council



As part of our ongoing efforts to advance our services and develop our work as industry leaders, FEDSIM created the FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council. This group of industry leaders brings together members of the government contracting community to advise FEDSIM throughout our expansion and evolution. We envision this group as an invaluable feedback loop, that not only helps inform the direction of our organizational strategy, it is also a space for us to lead as conveners among the acquisition community.

Our FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council has four committees that guide the initiatives and strategy of the FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council. Each committee is self-governed and works to drive the work of the FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council.
