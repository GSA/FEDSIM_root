FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council

FEDSIM created the FEDSIM Industry Advisory Council (FIAC) to ensure ongoing communication with industry leaders. The FIAC brings together members of the government contracting community to advise FEDSIM and provide an invaluable feedback loop within the federal acquisition community.

The FIAC has three committees that work together to guide the organizations.


FIAC Committees

  • Streamlined Acquisition
  • Small Business
  • Marketing/Outreach
