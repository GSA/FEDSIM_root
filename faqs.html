Frequently Asked Questions

What are the typical projects FEDSIM supports?

 
Traditionally, FEDSIM focuses on complex acquisitions. Our acquisition and program management staff has a deep understanding of IT and professional services that allows us to deliver high-quality solutions.

Will you provide/assign a contracting officer's representative (COR) if one is not available at the requesting agency?

 
Yes. FEDSIM will provide/assign a COR as part of our service.

Will you write the SOW/SOO/PWS for the requesting agency?

Is the requesting agency responsible for providing a technical evaluation team?

 
Yes. The requesting agency is responsible for designating the technical evaluation team.

Is there a fee associated with Assisted Acquisition Services?

 
Yes. The assisted acquisition fee is based on each individual acquisition.

Where can I find more information about the Defense Procurement and Acquisition Policy?
