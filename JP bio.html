Jim PichÉ | Homeland Sector Director, FEDSIM

As the Homeland Sector Director for FEDSIM, Jim is responsible for the management and administration of FEDSIM's IT Projects and Professional Services Projects at DHS and its component agencies. Most notably the FEDSIM and DHS partnership to establish the CDM/CMaaS Blanket Purchase Agreement as a means to offer state-of-the-art cybersecurity tools and services to government networks and the Technology Integration Program (TIP) at St. Elizabeth’s campus – the future home of DHS.

Prior to his assignment at FEDSIM, Mr. Piché was the Customer Service Director for Navy ERP. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering from the U.S. Coast Guard Academy.
