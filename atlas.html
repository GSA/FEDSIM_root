Overview of Program

GSA is conducting market research on an exciting new opportunity called ATLAS (Acquisition Techniques for Land, Air, and Sea). ATLAS will support operations, readiness, maintenance, integration, and development of manned, optionally manned, and unmanned platforms for land, air, sea, and space as well as those functions that support these platforms. A sampling of the activities being considered for ATLAS are presented below:

  • Product Development, Deployment and Sustainment
  • Operations, Planning, Execution, Processing, & Dissemination
  • Readiness, Maintenance, & Logistics
  • Rapid Technology Integration
  • Technology Incubator
  • Ancillary Support Products & Services

The ATLAS team welcomes feedback from Government and industry partners to ensure if ATLAS moves forward, the solution meets the needs of the Government and increases participation from industry in this constantly evolving marketplace.

For more information please contact atlas@gsa.gov
