Overview

 
FEDSIM is a Client Support Center housed in the General Services Administration’s Office of Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS). FEDSIM is the leading provider of assisted acquisition and contract management for federal government agencies. We provide hands-on acquisition, financial, and project management to ensure successful and timely acquisitions. Using repeatable and efficient protocols, we provide measurable steps and strategies across the life cycle of each contract to ensure that our client achieves their goals at each major acquisition milestone.

Mission Statement

 
To build innovative acquisition solutions that empower federal agencies.

Vision Statement

 
To transform the federal acquisition process.
