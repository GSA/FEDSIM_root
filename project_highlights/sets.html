U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS)

Project: St. Elizabeths Technology Services (SETS)

Dollar Value: $628 Million Ceiling


The United States Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is consolidating DHS Headquarters and Component Agencies that are located in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area (i.e., National Capital Region [NCR]) to the historic West St. Elizabeths Campus located in southeast Washington, D.C. It is DHSâ€™ intent to move approximately 17,000 employees occupying 12,800 seats onto the St. Elizabeths Campus by Fiscal Year 2022. The purpose of this task order is to provide professional Information Technology (IT) services to design, procure, configure, implement, test, secure, accredit, operate, and maintain a shared IT infrastructure and services for the St. Elizabeths Campus.
