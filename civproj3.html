Client: U.S. DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE, UNITED STATES MARSHALS SERVICE

Project: MISSION MODERNIZATION

Dollar Value: $232 MILLION

FEDSIM was approached by the United States Marshals Service (USMS) to join an agency-wide modernization program. FEDSIM crafted an acquisition strategy to facilitate the build out of a highly integrated and mobile IT system to address the challenges faced by USMS in the 21st century. Through the use of a Cost Plus Award Fee (CPAF) Alliant GWAC task order, FEDSIM created a flexible partnership with industry that has allowed for an agile development process via a cloud based solution. This performance based acquisition solution serves as a pilot program for cloud implementation at USMS and the Department of Justice, and will spur further innovation and cost savings through cloud adoption.
