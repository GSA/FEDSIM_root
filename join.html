FEDSIM is the leading provider of assisted acquisition services and contract management for federal agencies. We help them complete complex acquisitions of IT systems and services, professional services, and other resources they need to achieve their missions.



We’re looking for creative, detail-oriented people with expertise in acquisition, financial, or project management. Our unique culture comprises teams of:

Innovators

Our agile team develops new processes and tools to streamline acquisitions for our clients.

Problem Solvers

We navigate complex regulations and provide creative solutions customized for each project.

Experts

Our staff has extensive, varied expertise in federal acquisitions and project management.

Client Champions

We are client advocates—our primary focus is to help agencies achieve their critical missions.

Career Paths at FEDSIM

Acquisition

Contracting Specialists and Contracting Officers are solution brokers, leaders, and quality managers. They leverage their knowledge of complex acquisition policy to pinpoint creative, contractually compliant solutions that solve each client’s unique challenges.

Program Management

Project Managers and Acquisition Project Managers facilitate client projects to ensure that industry partners deliver high-quality solutions on time and on budget. They are customer advocates—they ensure customer engagement and satisfaction throughout the project life cycle.
