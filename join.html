FEDSIM is the leading provider of assisted acquisition services and contract management for federal agencies. We help clients meet their missions with complex IT systems, services, and professional services acquisitions.



We’re looking for creative, detail-oriented people with expertise in acquisition, financial, or project management. Our unique culture comprises teams of:

Collaborators

Our staff produces exceptional results because we work as a team to navigate complex regulations and provide creative solutions customized for each project.

Innovators

We remove traditional obstacles by operating as a business to build novel acquisition solutions that empower federal agencies.

Experts

FEDSIM keeps its promises: mission execution, client service, transparency, and product delivery. Integrity shows in all aspects of our work.

Client Champions

We are client advocates first and foremost. Our primary focus is to help our clients achieve their critical missions successfully.

Career Paths at FEDSIM

Acquisition

Acquisition Project Managers, Contract Specialists and Contracting Officers are solution brokers, leaders, and quality managers. They leverage their knowledge of complex acquisition policy to pinpoint creative, contractually compliant solutions that solve each client’s unique challenges.

Project Management

Project Managers facilitate client projects to ensure that industry partners deliver high-quality solutions on time and on budget. They are customer advocates—they ensure customer engagement and satisfaction throughout the project life cycle.
