Career Paths at FEDSIM

Acquisition

Acquisition Project Managers, Contract Specialists and Contracting Officers are solution brokers, leaders, and quality managers. They leverage their knowledge of complex acquisition policy to pinpoint creative, contractually compliant solutions that solve each client’s unique challenges.

Project Management

Project Managers facilitate client projects to ensure that industry partners deliver high-quality solutions on time and on budget. They are client advocates—they ensure client engagement and satisfaction throughout the project life cycle.
